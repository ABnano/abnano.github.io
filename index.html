I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Université catholique de Louvain (UCLouvain), specializing in Bio-Integrated AI, nanomaterials, and machine learning applications. My research focuses on cutting-edge innovations in healthcare diagnostics and electronic devices. Currently, I am working on the deployment of generative AI models, particularly Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) and Diffusion Models, for the discovery of novel material structures. I have completed my Ph.D. degree from the Institute of Nano Science and Technology (INST)-Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Mohali. My doctoral thesis mainly focuses on the fabrication of piezo-, tribo- and flexo-electric sensors and mechanical energy harvesters and studying their nanoscale properties, 3D printing, self-powered biomedical devices, and acoustic sensors, including IoT and IoMT technologies, and integrating them with machine learning algorithms.
