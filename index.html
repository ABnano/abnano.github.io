About Me

Anand Babu
Postdoc Researcher · UCLouvain
547Citations
12h-index
13i10

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at UCLouvain, Belgium, deploying generative AI models — VAEs and Diffusion Models — for multimodal machine learning and inverse design of materials.

I completed my Ph.D. at INST-IISER Mohali, India, building AI-integrated piezo-, tribo-, and flexo-electric sensors for digital health. As a Raman-Charpak Fellow at IMS, University of Bordeaux, I developed Voiceprint Biometrics, e-skin Respirometers, and Emotion Sensing devices.

My research spans AI for Materials, MLIP, Self-Driving Laboratories, Quantum ML, and Wearable Sensor Systems. I serve on the Editorial Advisory Board of APL Machine Learning and as Guest Editor for Nanotechnology (IOP).

Ph.D. Thesis Bio-Integrated Artificial Intelligence: Synergizing Nanomaterials, Machine Learning Algorithms, and Self-Powered Devices for Early-Stage Healthcare Diagnostics
