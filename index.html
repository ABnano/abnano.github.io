Anand Babu
Postdoc Researcher · UCLouvain
547Citations

13i10

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Université catholique de Louvain (UCLouvain), Belgium, where I deploy generative AI models — particularly Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) and Diffusion Models — for multimodal machine learning and inverse design of materials.

I completed my Ph.D. at INST-IISER Mohali, India, focusing on piezo-, tribo-, and flexo-electric sensors integrated with AI/ML algorithms for digital health applications. During my doctoral research, as a Raman-Charpak Fellow at IMS, University of Bordeaux, I developed Voiceprint Biometrics, e-skin Respirometers, and Emotion Sensing devices.

My current research interests span AI for Materials, Machine Learning Interatomic Potentials (MLIP), Self-Driving Laboratories, Quantum Machine Learning, and Wearable Sensor Systems. I serve on the Editorial Advisory Board of APL Machine Learning (AIP) and as a Guest Editor for Nanotechnology (IOP Science).
